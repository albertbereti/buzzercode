Buzzercode Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Buzzercode, we collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We also collect usage data such as call duration, connection times, and platform interactions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the Buzzercode service, process payments and wallet transactions, connect you with creators, send service-related notifications, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Payment Data

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, our third-party payment processor. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with service providers necessary to operate the platform (payment processing, hosting, analytics), when required by law, or with your explicit consent.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and analyze platform usage through Google Analytics. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Call metadata is retained for 90 days for billing and dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also request a copy of your data or object to certain processing activities. Contact us at privacy@buzzercode.com to exercise these rights.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), secure authentication, and regular security audits to protect your data.

9. Children's Privacy

Buzzercode is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notification. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@buzzercode.com.
